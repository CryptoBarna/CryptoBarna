~ ABOUT ~


WHY CREATE THIS?

When some cypherpunks came to Barcelona many years ago we were frustrated at the difficulty of finding information on what services and communities e isted locally to interact with.

What we found were ‘blockchain consultancy companies’ or inactive meetup groups that were only discussing one blockchain or project (silos of knowledge).

We believe that this space should be inspired from the movements that came before it and seek to be open and accessible to all.

We wanted an open and permissionless network – one that covers all blockchains, tokens, projects, and applications of the technology – one that anyone can freely join. The seed was planted to create what is now CryptoBarna.

As supporters of FOSS and CC licensing we chose to create this website. To make it accessible, translated into three languages and free to use for all users in the network.

On the eve of the 13th anniversary of the Bitcoin Whitepaper we started building, a poc a poc.


WHY IS IT CALLED CRYPTOBARNA?

That is because ‘Crypto‘ refers to cryptography, not only a core technology of cryptocurrencies and blockchains but is also for encrypted communications & IT systems.

The second part of it is ‘ Barna‘ which is the colloquial and legally accepted abbreviation of Barcelona and is used by locals to refer to their city. That said his website covers the whole region.

No monetisation of the content or platform is planned. This service is a not-for-profit act by enthusiasts of the technology for the benefit of other users and theecosystem.

All running costs are paid in full at this time, it is a gift to you and only to be used for educational purposes.
